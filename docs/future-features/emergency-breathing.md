# Emergency Breathing Feature — Future Build

## Overview
60-second emergency breathing exercise accessible from anywhere in the app.
5-6 breaths per cycle (resonance frequency: ~6 breaths/min).
Timing: 4s inhale, 2s hold, 4s exhale = 10s/cycle x 6 = 60s.

## Selected Direction: "The Ripple" (Option A)
Gold stroke rings pulse outward from center on inhale, settle back on exhale.
Each ring fades as it expands — breath becoming waves moving into the world.
Metaphor: A single voice creates ripples of impact. Your calm radiates outward.

### Why Ripple wins for FieldVoices
- Direct metaphor for amplifying voices
- Stroke-based rings match existing icon language
- Gold (#C9A84C) on navy (#0A0F1C) is premium
- Can extend existing `logo-breathe` keyframe
- Pure CSS implementation, performant

### Bonus Layer: "Breathing Together"
Small line: "47 others breathing right now" (estimated count).
Transforms solitary coping into communal act. Research shows even suggestion
of group breathwork enhances calming and reduces isolation.

## Alternative Concepts (saved for future tools)

### Option B: "The Waveform" — Calm Your Voice
Audio waveform visualization that breathes. On inhale, wave amplitude grows.
On exhale, it settles toward stillness. Unique — no other wellness app uses this.
Direct brand connection: the waveform IS a voice finding calm.

### Option C: "The Gathering" — Fireflies Coming Home
Gold particles drift on navy background. On inhale, they gather inward (community
forming). On exhale, they release outward (carrying light back into the world).
Subtle connection lines between nearby particles = community bonds.
Warm, organic, never repeats exactly.

### Option D: "The Bloom" — Organic Petal Unfolding
Apple Watch Breathe-inspired but reimagined with sage/gold palette.
Overlapping organic shapes unfold on inhale, fold closed on exhale.
Nature/growth metaphor.

### Option E: "The Aurora" — Flowing Gradient Light
Slow-moving luminous gradient bands (northern lights) that intensify on inhale,
dim on exhale. Navy/gold/sage palette as flowing light. "Expensive but lightweight."

### Option F: "Community Pulse" — Shared Rhythm
Abstract rhythmic visualization with "others breathing with you" counter.
